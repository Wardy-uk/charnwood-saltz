Dive St. Abbs - http://www.divestabbs.info

From this tiny, ancient and picturesque harbour on the eastern coast of the Scottish Borders, Dive St. Abbs enables divers to explore the sea, fishermen to catch fish and everyone to see the amazing geology, exciting history and wonderful wildlife and birds to be found along this spectacular coastline.

 

'Rhovanion B&B - http://www.rhovanion.org

Fiona and Michael Clift welcome you to Rhovanion Bed & Breakfast in Coldingham, the perfect location for a relaxing break. We offer en-suite bed and breakfast accommodation in a pleasant rural setting in a quiet Coldingham lane alongside a pretty 'burn' and just a short walk from the award winning beach at Coldingham Bay.

Jack Ingle - http://www.jackingle.co.uk

Jack Ingle has been diving since 1980 and led many Technical Diving expeditions.

Some of these include dives on the Andrea Dorea (USA), HMS Repulse and HMS Prince of Wales in the South China Seas and the USS Wilkes Barre in Florida.
