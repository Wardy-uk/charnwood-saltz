Social feed plugin for displaying your facebook posts, Instagram Posts & Twitter Posts in a feed on your website. 

== Description ==

Want to show what's going on facebook time-line then you are in right place , Facebook Feed is the plugin to display public facebook feeds on your site with lots of customization options.  

Facebook Feed is a WordPress plugin that displays Facebook, Twitter and Instagram feed of your pages on your website. Facebook feed is very easy to use and in few minutes you can show any public facebook page feed on your website. Each social network can be signed into individually and there are several options to customize the displayed feed. 

Display a completely responsive & customizable facebook feed on your website which match with the look and feel of your website.

Combine your Facebook, Twitter & Instagram feeds into one stream with Facebook feed plugin easily.

Facebook feed plugin supports super easy User Interface for you to easily add facebook,twitter & Instagram feeds.

Facebook feed plugin have multiple themes to choose from. With complete customizabilty of colors, fonts & size your facebook, instagram & twitter feed will make your website more engaging & entertaining.

Facebook plugin is shortcode supported which allows you to display your facebook, instagram & twitter feeds anywhere on your website.

Facebook Feed Plugin doesn't expose your private details (like tokens, app IDs and app secrets) to browser and keeps it on server so we provide 100% security for any sensitive data.

Facebook feed plugin also supports instagram feed to display charming instagram pictures.

Facebook feed plugin allows you limit the number of results per feed so you can display your latest posts only.

Facebook feed plugin allows you to hide text content, if you want to display only pictures from facebook or twitter this feature will help you.

Facebook feed plugin multiple feed layouts to display your Facebook, Twitter & Instagram feeds easily in different ways. 

Facebook feed plugin allows you to enable links to posts for your visitors if they want to view the full post or your page on facebok.

Facebook feed items can have titles and/or descriptions with various options of showing them based on item display style.

With facebook feed plugin you can combine unlimited social feeds and create unlimited number of social streams.

Quickly & easily customize facebook, twitter & instagram feed colors via color pickers in plugin admin page.

Stream posts include twitter, facebook, Instagram. Load items in chunks which can either be loaded manually or automatically as the page is scrolled.

Facebook feed plugin supports Google fonts for your Facebook, Twitter & Instagram feed. You can display/hide facebook icon.

== Installation ==

The easy way :

1. Go to the Plugins Menu in WordPress.
1. Search for plugin "Add Facebook".
1. Click "Install".
1. After Installation click activate to start using the coming soon plugin.

* Go to Facebook feed from Dashboard menu.
* Enable Facebook feed feature.


Not so easy way : 

1. To install facebook feed via FTP
1. Download the facebook feed
1. Unarchive facebook feed plugin
1. Copy folder with add-facebook.zip
1. Open the ftp \wp-content\plugins\
1. Paste the plug-ins folder in the folder
1. Go to admin panel => open item "Plugins" => activate facebook feed.

* Go to Facebook feed from Dashboard menu.
* Enable Facebook feed feature.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Facebook Feed Frequently Asked Questions =

<strong>What does facebook feed plugin do ?</strong>

A. Facebook feed plugin allows you to create feeds of your social media pages (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) and display them in a stream of posts. Facebook feed plugin allows design customizability to display your facebook posts beatifully. Facebook feed is easy to use and require no technical knowledge to setup.

<strong>Why do I need a Feed of my social media pages (Facebok, Twitter, Instagram) on my website ? </strong>

A. In today's world social media marketing is every thing, Facebook is first thing most people check in the morning. In a world which is dominated by social media giants like facebook twitter & instagram you can give your visitors a view of your social media campaign on your website which if they like could result in larger social media (Facebook , Twitter & Instagram) following. People follow quality.

<strong> Q. How to add new Facebook Feed to your WordPress site  ? </strong>

A. After activating the Facebook Feed plugin, you'll see the Facebook Feed menu in WordPress menu, click on it and you'll see the Add New Facebook Feed button on the top of the Facebook Feeds Page.


<strong> Q. How to Facebook Feed ? </strong>

A. To enable Facebook Feed plugin, create a new Facebook Feed by going to Facebook Feed menu from your WordPress admin and click on the Add New Facebook Feed button, under Social Media tab, you can enable or disable your desired feed.


<strong> Q. From where I get my Facebook Profile/Page ID ? </strong>

A. To get your facebook profile/page id, go in to your facebook account from any desktop browser and go into your profile, you see you facebook profile/page id from there.


<strong> Q. How to get Facebook App ID ? </strong>

A. To your facebook ID you need to create an app. Follow instruction in this tutorial to get your Facebook ID http://www.shoutmeloud.com/get-facebook-app-id-secret-key.html.


<strong> Q. How to get Facebook Secret App ID ? </strong>

A. To get your facebook secret app id. Follow instruction in this tutorial to get your Secret App ID http://www.shoutmeloud.com/get-facebook-app-id-secret-key.html 


<strong> Q. How to show limited number of feeds ? </strong>

A. You can select how many facebook feed should show, to do that you need to go into your previously created feeds and edit your desired feed. You'll find Results Per Feed option there.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide date of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the date for your Facebook Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Posted On Date.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide Social Media Icons of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the Social Media Icons for your Facebook Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Social Media Icon.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide Display Picture of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the Display Picture for your Facebook Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Display Picture.


<strong> Q. How to show Facebook Feed in Post/Page ? </strong>

A. To show Facebook Feed in your Post/Page, copy the provided shortcode into your Post where you want to show your Facebook Feed.




<strong> Q. How to show Facebook Feed in your Widget Area ? </strong>

A. To show Facebook Feed in your Widget Area, go into your Widget area and find the Text Widget, add the Text Widget into your Sidebar area and paste the provided shortcode.




=  PREMIUM VERSION FAQs  =


<strong> Q. How to increase or decrease the facebook feed container width ? </strong>

A. To increase or decrease the width of your facebook feed container, go into your Facebook Feed settings, Under the Facebook Feed Settings, you'll find the option to change the width. Enter the width in pixels.


<strong> Q. How to hide the description of a feed and show only images ? </strong>

A. To hide the description of your feed, option Facebook Feed settings, Under the Social Media Settings you'll find the Hide Text Content Option to show or hide the description.


<strong> Q. How to enable/disable the link to post feature ? </strong>

A. To enable/disable the link to post feature, go into your Facebook Feed settings, Under the Social Media Settings tab, you'll find the Enable / Disable Links to Post option.


<strong> Q. How to change the background color of your Facebook Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the background color of your Facebook Feed, go into your Facebook Feed settings, Under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Background Color option.


<strong> Q. How to change the Text Color of your Facebook Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the text color of your Facebook Feed, go into your Facebook Feed settings, Under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Text Color option.


<strong> Q. How to change the Text Font of your Facebook Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the Text Font of your Facebook Feed, go into your Facebook Feed settings, Under the Facebook Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Text Font option.
