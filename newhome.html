Welcome to Charnwood Saltz S.A.C

Charnwood based B.S.A.C. affiliated scuba diving club.

About Us

We are a small B.S.A.C. affiliated diving club located in the heart of Chanrnwood. We were formed in 1977 and have been training local people to dive ever since.


If you are new to diving or have a BSAC or Padi qualification and want to further your diving with a small, friendly club get in touch.

Contact Us

We meet most Wednesday evenings at King Edward VII Science & Sport College's Coalville swimming pool (term time only) from 19:30 to 20:30.


If you would like to come and meet us or arrange a try dive please get in touch. Please note: during the warmer months, we are often at Stoney Cove on the 1st and 3rd Wednesday's of the month.
